Great Fantasy and Sci-Fi Books for Teen Girls

Learn about books!

Being a science fiction lover myself, I have read many good sci-fi books over the course of my life and have come across several I would recommend to others, such as those on the list I have compiled below.

  • Harry Potter- This series is probably one of the best known fantasy series around ,and it definately deserves it's fame (at least in my opinion). These books are a must-read for all fantasy lovers.
  • The Lord of the Rings & The Hobbit- It is true that these books are harder reads then most, but it is definately worth your while to read them, trust me.
  • Fablehaven & Dragonwatch- Fablehaven and it's sequel series, Dragonwatch, do a fantastic job of combinding the world of make-believe creatures and magic with the real one. This collection of books is a great family read-aloud as well as a great series for solo reading too.
  • Percy Jackson - These books make a great series for those who are interested in Greek mythology but still appreciate modern aspects in the books they read as well.
  • The Five Kingdoms- Anyone who likes diverse action and settings in the books they read will simply adore this series. The books are perfect for practically everyone!
  • The Hunger Games - This trilogy is action packed and will leave you both enthralled and desperate to read more. These are excellent books for those who love adventure books as well as doomsday settings.
  • The Inheritance Cycle - The adventuresome setting of the books will leave you spellbound, even after you have finished reading them.
  • The Dragonlance Chronicles- For all those who appreciate dragons, elves, wizards, and others of the sort this series is a perfect match. The unpredictability of these books will make them page-turners for almost anyone.
  • The Wheel of Time - Everything about this series is full of adventure and action. The many epic battles and quests these books include will leave you entranced.
  • The Mazerunner- This series will have you on the edge of your seat with all of it's cliffhangers and action-filled pages. You will find yourself having a hard time simply putting these apocalyptic-themed books down when you try to stop.
